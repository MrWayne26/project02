Batman

Batman is a superhero who appears in American comic books published by DC Comics. He is the Dark Knight, the Caped Crusader, and the World's Greatest Detective.

The Joker

The Joker is a supervillain created by Bill Finger, Bob Kane, and Jerry Robinson who first appeared in the debut issue of the comic book Batman (April 25, 1940).
